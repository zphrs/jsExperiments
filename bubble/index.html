Attributes

elem-bubbling - Selector for the element that will be bubbled.
transition-speed - The speed of the animation.
return-position - Where the bubble eventually comes to rest.
bubble-size - The radius of the bubble.
smooth - If the element should be animated on initial load to avoid a potential flash.
match-height - Makes the direct children of the bubble-elem the width and height of the input element.
Ex. <bubble-elem elem-bubbling=".bubbled" transition-speed="1.5s" return-position="50% 18%" bubble-size="19%" smooth>
<div class="bubbled"></div>
</bubble-elem>

About the bubble movement

The bubble will expand to the size of the "elem-bubbling" element. It will expand away from where your mouse entered the element
Then when the user moves the mouse out of the element, the bubble will contract to double the "bubble-size"
Then the bubble will return to the "return-position" and contract to the "bubble-size."
