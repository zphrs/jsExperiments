So if you wanna get to know us keep scrolling!

Definition

Being bi, strictly speaking, means that I like-like more than one gender.

Misconceptions

Contrary to popular belief, being bi does not necessarily mean that I like only 2 genders, as the definition has evolved to be more gender-inclusive.

Additionally I'm not "just confused" about my sexuality.

Most people who identify as bi do genuinely know that they get butterflies for more than one gender sometimes.

Infidelity

The misconception that a bi person cannot be satisfied with having only one partner in their love lives is false.

On average, bisexuals from the study find that monogamy is slightly more enhancing to a relationship than it is a sacrifice for a relationship.

Additionally, "78.4% of bi-identified men and 67% of bi-identified women were either seriously dating one person, engaged, or married."

I am by no means saying that every bi person is perfect, but the fact that we are open to dating more than one gender does not mean that we are intrinsically less loyal or unable to have a long-term relationship.
